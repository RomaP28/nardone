About Us

Welcome to Nardone Home Repair and Restoration!

I'm Tony Nardone, founder and heartbeat of this venture, alongside my loving and supportive wife, Natalia. Named after our family, our company is a testament to our shared dedication to craftsmanship, precision, and service. Rooted deeply in family values, our operations emphasize trust, commitment, and community connections.

With over two decades of navigating the complexities of construction and management, our expertise sets us apart. My 14 years at Universal Orlando in facilities and construction management not only honed my skills but also ignited an unwavering passion for craftsmanship. This longstanding journey constantly invigorates our love for the craft.

Seeing an opportunity in adversity, Natalia and I decided to channel our experiences and skills into Nardone Home Repair and Restoration. This venture wasn't just a business decision; it was a commitment to our community, a promise to bring our combined experiences to households and businesses needing repair, restoration, and a touch of Nardone craftsmanship. Our story is not just about building and repairing. It's about resilience, reinvention, and commitment to quality. Whether you're looking to breathe new life into a space or need expert repair solutions, we're here, bringing with us a legacy of dedication and precision.

At Nardone Home Repair and Restoration, we don't merely fix and build; we create, restore, and solidify relationships in our beloved Orlando community.

With Nardone Home Repair and Restoration your satisfaction is guaranteed!
